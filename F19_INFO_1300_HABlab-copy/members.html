mc47@cornell.edu

Matthew C. Velasco is Co-Director of the Human and Animal Bone Lab and Assistant Professor in the Department of Anthropology at Cornell University. He specializes in the bioarchaeological analysis of skeletal and mummified human remains from highland Peru. His current project in Peru, Proyecto Collaguas Antiguos (PCA), investigates how social and ethnic identity shaped health outcomes during the pre-Inka period (AD 1100-1450).
