2016

Mr. Krabs Blur

Source

Origin:

This meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "Patty Hype," in which 46,853 angry customers demand that Mr.Krabs give them a refund for the negative side effects of the new "Pretty Patties."

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to depict that one is confused, scared, out of their comfort zone, or forced into the limelight.

Patrick's Wallet

Source

Origin:

This meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy III," in which Manray tries to perform a good deed by returning Patrick's wallet. Patrick, however, wants no part of the good deed!

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized in order to make a point about literally anything-- from something trivial to even a political point-- through the rationalization aspect of this meme.

Dabbing Squidward

Source

Origin:

This meme actually did not originate from the show, and it's not a meme either-- it's a vine! The hilarity behind this vine is that an amusement park worker, at Universal Park Orlando, dabbed on request while wearing a Squidward costume "(Dab Squidward)." Despite of the fact that this is neither an in-show reference nor a meme, this vine is just as popular as the Spongebob memes.

Applicability:

This vine isn't really used in the same ways that memes are; this vine is typically only used for a good laugh.
