2015

Caveman Spongebob

Source

Origin:

This meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "SB-129," in which Squidward goes back in time in search of some "alone time"-- away from Spongebob and Patrick. In a twist of events, however, he encounters their ancestors!

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to show that one is in a fight-or-flight scenario, or a situation that calls for immediate action.

"I'll Have You Know"

Source

Origin:

This meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "No Weenies Allowed," in which SpongeBob tries to prove how "tough" he is by telling a story of how he only cried for "only 20 minutes after stubbing [his] toe." Needless to say, this does not impress the bouncer of the Salty Spitoon-- a tough-guy establishment.

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to describe an unfortunate situation that one has gotten themselves in. This meme template usually begins with "I'll have you know" and ends with "And I only cried for 20 minutes."

Sleeping Squidward

Source

Origin:

This meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "Home Sweet Pineapple," in which Squidward suddenly realizes that SpongeBob is not only inside of his house, but sleeping next to him as well! The image in the meme captures that hilarious moment of realization that we can all relate to in our lives!

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to express shock in realization to the assigned task/s that one forgot to fulfill.
