Increasingly Buff Spongebob

Source

Origin:

This meme actually originates from two different Spongebob episodes from top to bottom, respectively: "Not Normal" and "The Fry Cook Games." The top image shows Spongebob's attempt at being "normal" and not his "annoying self" in order to appease Squidward. Meanwhile, the latter two images depict various parts from the latter episode in which Spongebob gets serious about defeating Patrick and winning the Fry Cook Games.

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to humorously exagerate how someone or something's power levels and/or intensity increases given varying circumstances.

"Ight Imma Head Out"

Source

Origin:

This meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "The Smoking Peanut." It captures the exact moment in which SpongeBob goes to sit down; this meme, however, portrays Spongebob as if he were just about to leave.

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to express one's desire to escape from a situation that he or she wants no part of.

Laughing Spongebob

Source

Origin:

This meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "New Student Starfish," in which SpongeBob takes Patrick to boating school with him and they both start laughing at silly jokes, such as "I thought of something funnier than 24... 25!" This meme also consists of Enel's face-- from One Piece-- being plastered on Spongebob's face.

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to depict double standards and hypocrisy underlying controversial subjects and situations.
