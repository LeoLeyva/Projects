Spongebob Crowd

Source

Origin:

The left part of this meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "BubbleStand," while the right part of this meme is from the episode of "Patty Hype." This meme consists of different parts taken from different episodes of Spongebob Squarepants in order to deliver its intended effect.

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to get a point across of how significant the addition of what's inserted in the bottom left of the meme is for hyping up the "product" that is trying to be sold on the top left of the meme.

Mocking Spongebob

Source

Origin:

This meme originated from a scene in the Spongebob episode of "Little Yellow Book," in which the content of Spongebob's diary reveals his instinctual response to seeing plaid: he acts like a chicken.

Applicability:

This meme is typically utilized to taunt people who have ideas/beliefs that one does not usually agree with. The impression that this meme is supposed to give off is, "I know you are but what am I?"
